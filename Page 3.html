Using a Sony Bluray Player

Sony UBX-300 Instructional Manual

Compatibility

  • Blu-Ray discs, DVD discs will be the only compatible discs that will function in the player.
  • 4K, MP3, and VCD discs will not function in this Blu-Ray Player.
  • Discs will need to be in good working order to be able to play in the system.

Region Codes

Each Blu-Ray player and Blu-Ray disc or DVD disc will be region locked by the manufacturer of the Blu-Ray player. This prevents piracy and locks the Blu-Rays and DVDs sold at designated locations at different prices by manufacturers.

  • Region A refers to the North American region.
  • Region B refers to Japan, Europe, South Africa, the Middle East
  • Region C refers to Southeast Asia, and East Asia

Inserting Discs Into the Player

Press the eject button located on the front of the Blu-Ray player, shaped like an arrow with an underline. A tray will open for an opening to hold the disc. Insert Blu-Ray or DVD discs face side up, typically the side printed white or with design will be the face side. To return the tray with the disc into the Blu-Ray player, do one of the following.

Set-Up

The power cord must be plugged in the back with the matching color code. HDMI cables are required for audio/visual compatibility across various devices. There are 2 ports, one for audio, and one for visual and audio. There is a Coaxial jack which can be used to play audio to external devices. A USB port is available for cross device connectivity and sharing information across devices. Firmly press the head of the wires into the designated ports on the Blu-Ray Player; refer to the manual for the location of the ports. If the power code is successfully plugged into the port and the power button is subsequently pressed, the Player will light up.
